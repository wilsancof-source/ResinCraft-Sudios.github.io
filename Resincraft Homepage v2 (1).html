05 / Our Process

SIX STEPS. ONE STANDARD.
EXCELLENCE.

01
SITE ASSESSMENT

On-site evaluation of substrate, moisture levels, load requirements, and project scope. No assumptions — only data.

02
SURFACE PREP

Diamond grinding, shot blasting, crack repair, and moisture mitigation. The foundation of every flawless coat.

03
PRIMER COAT

Penetrating epoxy primer bonds at molecular level. Zero pinholes. Eliminates delamination risk permanently.

04
SYSTEM INSTALL

Main coat application — industrial, anti-static, or decorative system installed to exact specification and DFT.

05
TOPCOAT & SEAL

UV-stable, chemical-resistant topcoat buffed and sealed. The layer the world sees — and judges.

06
QUALITY SIGN-OFF

Adhesion pull tests, DFT checks, and full inspection. We hand over keys only when every square metre passes.
