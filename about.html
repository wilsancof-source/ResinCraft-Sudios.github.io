What We Do

FULL-SYSTEM
EPOXY.
PROPERLY
DONE.

Resincraft Studios offers the complete range of high-performance flooring systems: metallic decorative epoxy for hospitality and retail, self-levelling industrial epoxy for warehouses and factories, anti-microbial systems for healthcare environments, anti-static ESD flooring for military and technology facilities, and heavy-duty epoxy mortar systems for food processing and chemical plants.

Every client comes to us with a surface that isn't performing the way it should. We listen first, assess properly, then design the right system for the actual conditions — not just the cheapest one that gets the job signed. If a simpler solution works, we'll say so. If the environment demands something more robust, we'll explain why.

Our team has worked on everything from small residential commissions to large port and military facilities. That range means we've encountered most problems before, and we know how to handle them without turning a straightforward project into a complicated one.

The Market Opportunity

A MARKET
THAT NEEDED
SOMEONE
TO DO IT RIGHT.

East Africa — and Djibouti specifically — is going through a construction and infrastructure investment surge unlike anything in its modern history. Billions of dollars are flowing into port expansion, industrial free zones, hospital construction, hotel development, and military base modernisation. All of it creates urgent demand for high-performance flooring.

Yet that demand has been served by two inadequate options: local contractors applying sub-standard water-based paint they market as 'epoxy flooring,' and expensive international firms that charge three to five times local market rates and have no permanent in-country presence. Neither option works for serious projects.

What We Stand For

We've built this company around a set of principles that don't change depending on the size of the job or the size of the client. These apply to every project we take on.

01

We inspect every substrate before we price anything. If there are problems, we tell you upfront — not halfway through the job. No surprises, no hidden costs.

How We Got Here

HOW WE
BUILT THIS.

Resincraft Studios didn't start with a big team or a large funding round. It started with a clear observation: the market needed a company that could do this properly. Here's how we got from that idea to where we are today.

01
WHERE IT STARTED

Resincraft Studios began in Djibouti with a small team and a clear focus: do epoxy flooring properly in a market where it was being done poorly. The first projects were industrial — warehouses and logistics facilities where floor quality has real consequences.

02
EXPANDING THE RANGE

We added decorative resin systems to the offering after seeing consistent demand from residential and hospitality clients who wanted something more than a standard industrial finish. The metallic and marble-effect floors quickly became a significant part of the business.

03
REGIONAL GROWTH

We started taking projects outside Djibouti — Ethiopia, Somalia, and the first UAE enquiries came in. Working across different climates and substrates forced us to sharpen our technical approach. Every market has its own challenges, and adapting to them made us better.

04
WHERE WE ARE NOW

Today we operate across East Africa and the Middle East, handling projects from small residential jobs to large-scale commercial and industrial installations. The team is larger, the portfolio is deeper, and the approach is the same as it was on day one: do it right.
